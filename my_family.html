My Family Background



My mother is a single mother who raises three children on her own. She used to work as an English teacher, a security guard at a supermarket, a cashier, and so on. To put it simply, my mother has worked hard to maintain our family of four since my father pass away. However, my mother has lately retired due to age. It amazes me how my mother is so strong and successful in raising her children alone.
My brother graduated from high school in 2015 at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Sungai Ara in Pulau Pinang, and continued his studies at Politeknik Sultan Abdul Halim Muadzam Shah in Jitra Kedah, which he finished in 2019. Now working as a technician at Analog Device Sdn Bhd.
My sister graduated from high school in 2016 at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Sungai Ara in Pulau Pinang. then continue her diploma studies at Kolej Profesional Mara Seri Iskandar in Perak, which she completed in 2020. She is now an undergraduate student at UITM Kedah.
