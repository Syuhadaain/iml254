My primary school

Sekolah Kebangsaan Sungai Ara

Address:Jalan Sungai Ara 1,11900 Bayan Lepas,Pulau Pinang,Malaysia.

History and Background

Sekolah Kebangsaan Sungai Ara, commonly known as SK Sungai Ara, is part of Malaysia's national primary school system, providing education for students from Year 1 to Year 6. The school follows the national curriculum set by the Malaysian Ministry of Education, which includes subjects such as Bahasa Malaysia, English, Mathematics, Science, and other co-curricular activities. The school has a rich history rooted in the local community of Sungai Ara. It was established to cater to the educational needs of the growing population in the Bayan Lepas area, which has seen significant development over the years. SK Sungai Ara aims to provide a holistic education, focusing not only on academic excellence but also on the personal development and character building of its students.

I attended Sekolah Kebangsaan Sungai Ara from 2010 until 2016, a significant period that shaped much of my early childhood. The school, nestled in the vibrant community of Sungai Ara in Pulau Pinang, was where I spent my formative years from Year 1 to Year 6. Despite the school's excellent reputation and the many friends I made, my time there was challenging. I faced bullying from some of my classmates, which made going to school a daily struggle. The teasing and exclusion took a toll on my confidence and sense of belonging. Unfortunately, the difficulties didn't stop there. A few teachers, who should have been my mentors and protectors, also contributed to my distress by treating me unfairly, which further deepened my sense of isolation



My high school

Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Raja Tun Uda

Address:Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Raja Tun Uda, Jalan Sungai Nibong, 11900 Bayan Lepas, Pulau Pinang, Malaysia.

History and Background

Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Raja Tun Uda (SMK Raja Tun Uda) is a secondary school located in the bustling area of Bayan Lepas on Penang Island, Malaysia. The school is known for its commitment to academic excellence, holistic education, and co-curricular achievements. It caters to students from Form 1 to Form 5, offering a range of subjects and extracurricular activities to foster the intellectual and personal growth of its students. Named after Raja Tun Uda, a prominent Malaysian political figure and the first Governor of Penang, the school has a rich history of serving the local community. Since its establishment, SMK Raja Tun Uda has grown both in size and reputation. The school prides itself on creating a nurturing environment where students are encouraged to excel in academics, sports, and other co-curricular pursuits.

I attended Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Raja Tun Uda from 2017 to 2021, where I made many lasting friendships and enjoyed a variety of enriching experiences. The vibrant school community and supportive teachers made my time there memorable. We often went on exciting trips, such as our visit to the Penang Bird Park, which were both fun and educational, fostering a strong sense of camaraderie among us students. During my final years at the school, the COVID-19 pandemic necessitated a shift to online classes. This transition was challenging, but it taught me resilience and adaptability. Despite missing the lively school environment, we continued to learn and support each other through virtual means. My years at SMK Raja Tun Uda provided a solid foundation for my future, combining academic growth with personal development and unforgettable memories.



My university

Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Merbok, Kedah

Address: Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Kedah, 08400 Merbok, Kedah, Malaysia.

History and Background

Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) was originally established in 1956 as Dewan Latehan RIDA, with the primary aim of helping rural Malays through educational opportunities. It was later renamed several times, becoming MARA College and then Institut Teknologi MARA (ITM). In 1999, it gained university status and became Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM). The UiTM Kedah branch, located in Merbok, was established to expand the university's reach and provide educational opportunities to the northern region of Malaysia. The campus in Merbok is known for its scenic setting near the Sungai Petani area and the archaeological site of the ancient Bujang Valley, which adds a rich historical context to the location.

My journey at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Merbok began in 2022 when I embarked on my diploma studies in Library Informatics. Stepping onto the campus for the first time filled me with a mix of excitement and nervousness, unsure of what the next few years would hold. However, those initial uncertainties quickly faded as I immersed myself in the vibrant academic and social life of UiTM. During the first semester, I had the pleasure of meeting two incredible individuals who would become my closest friends throughout my time at UiTM. From shared classes to late-night study sessions and campus adventures, our bond grew stronger with each passing day. Together, we navigated the challenges of coursework, supported each other through tough times, and celebrated our achievements as a team.
